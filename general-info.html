General Info

Why this fair exists

Since 1957 the mission hasn’t moved an inch: raise money for the Lowcountry, show every family a great time doing it.

Three jobs, every year

  • 1. Raise money that stays local

    Every season funds grants for local charities, scholarships for students, and community needs across Berkeley, Charleston, and Dorchester counties.

  • 2. Family fun, full stop

    Every act, exhibit, and event is picked to be family-oriented entertainment the whole Lowcountry can enjoy — all ages, all eleven days.

  • 3. Connect sponsors to the crowd

    Corporate partners reach the region’s biggest annual audience — hundreds of thousands of attendees each season. Sponsorship details.

The Fair Gives Back

Where the money goes

One big check, ninety-plus recipients

After each fair, the Exchange Club of Charleston Foundation grants the proceeds out — $504,000+ to more than 90 local nonprofits and students after the latest season, $11 million+ since 2003.

Policies

The rules of the midway

  • Clear bag policy

    Clear bags only at the gates, with exceptions for medically necessary items (subject to inspection). Backpacks, coolers, and glass stay home.

  • Safety & conduct

    Security screening at every gate, a code of conduct that keeps the fair family-friendly, and no weapons or alcohol on the grounds — ever.

  • Service animals

    ADA service animals — trained dogs and miniature horses — are welcome. Pets and emotional support animals can’t come in.

  • Smoking

    Smoking and vaping are limited to designated areas. Look for signage or ask any staff member.
