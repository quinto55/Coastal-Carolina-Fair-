Directions & parking

  • From Charleston

    Take I-26 West to Exit 205A and merge onto US-78 West. Exchange Park is at 9850 Highway 78 — you’ll see it.

  • From Columbia

    Take I-26 East to Exit 203 (College Park Road) toward Ladson. Turn right on College Park Road, then right on US-78. Exchange Park is ahead on the left.

  • Parking

    On-site parking for 10,000+ cars, free with your visit. VIP parking at Gate 1 ($10 Mon–Fri / $20 Sat–Sun, online only) skips the vehicle lines — enter from Highway 78 only.

Before you pack

The short list

Clear bagsA clear bag policy is in effect at the gates. Medical-need exceptions are accommodated.

Leave at homeWeapons, alcohol, backpacks, glass, and coolers don’t come in.

Rain or shineThe fair runs in most weather; tickets aren’t refunded or rain-checked.
