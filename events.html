Events & Entertainment · 2026

All shows free with admission

No upcharges, no separate concert tickets. If it’s on a stage at the fair, your gate admission covers it.

What fills eleven days

2026 lineup coming late summer. Performers and daily schedules are announced closer to opening day. Until then, here’s what a fair week always holds — filter by what you’re into.

  • Headline entertainment

    Featured acts take the main stage throughout the run — announced with the 2026 lineup. Seating is first-come with your admission.

  • Parkway Stage

    Local bands, dance groups, and school ensembles perform daily. Want a slot? Apply to perform.

  • Strolling performers

    Roaming acts work the midway all day — you don’t find them, they find you.

  • Livestock & agriculture

    Cattle, goats, poultry, and more, shown and judged by Lowcountry youth and growers.

  • Home & craft competitions

    Baking, canning, quilting, art, and photography — ribbons on the line in the exhibit halls. Enter your best.

  • Live demonstrations

    Craftspeople and makers at work daily in the exhibit areas — watch, ask, learn.

  • Senior Day

    A mid-run weekday tradition: $5 admission for guests 55+, free caregiver admission, and dedicated morning programming from 10 AM to 2 PM. 2026 date announced with the schedule.

  • Heroes Days

    Free admission days honoring military members, veterans, and first responders — details on the deals page.
