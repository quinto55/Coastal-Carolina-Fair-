Tickets & Deals · 2026

Get in the gates

Everything included with admission, every way to save, and the only places you should ever buy a ticket.

2026 tickets

On sale soon. 2026 online ticket sales haven’t opened yet. When they do, this is where they’ll live — check back, or follow the fair on social for the announcement.

  • Buy official, always

    Tickets are valid only when purchased through the fair’s website or participating Circle K stores. Tickets bought from social media resellers can’t be verified — and often aren’t real.

  • What admission includes

    Every show and concert on the grounds is free with gate admission. Rides and games are separate — wristbands and credits are sold per day.

  • Good to know

    The fair runs rain or shine, and tickets aren’t refunded or rain-checked. Buy for a day you can make — with eleven to choose from, there’s room to plan.

  • VIP parking

    Skip the vehicle lines: $10 Mon–Fri, $20 Sat–Sun, sold online only, while spaces last. Park at Gate 1 — enter only from Highway 78. General parking stays free.
