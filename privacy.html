Privacy

Privacy Policy

The short version: this site collects nothing about you unless you say yes — and most of it collects nothing at all.

Policy details

1. Who we are

This website presents the Coastal Carolina Fair, held at Exchange Park, 9850 Highway 78, Ladson, SC 29456, and owned and operated by the Exchange Club of Charleston. Questions about this policy: 843.572.3161.

2. What this site collects by default

Nothing. Browsing this site requires no account, no form, and no personal information. Pages are static; reading them sends us nothing.

3. Cookies & analytics — only with your consent

If analytics are enabled on this site, a consent banner will ask before anything runs. Until you choose “Accept,” no analytics cookies are set and no measurement tools load. If you decline, they stay off. You can change your choice any time via the “Cookie preferences” link in the footer.

When accepted, the site may use Google Analytics (with IP anonymization) to understand visits, and a Meta pixel to measure how our promotions perform. Each sets its own cookies and processes data under its provider’s policies.

4. What runs without consent

One preference is stored in your browser’s local storage: your cookie choice itself, so we don’t ask again. Offline support caches pages on your device via a service worker. Neither sends anything to us or anyone else.

5. Calendar downloads

The “Add to calendar” buttons generate event files entirely on your device. Nothing about your calendar or your click leaves your browser.

6. Images & third-party content

Photographs and logos load from the fair’s content network. Standard web requests (your IP address, browser type) are visible to the servers hosting those images, as with any website. Links to external sites — ticket outlets, social media, the host hotel — are governed by those sites’ own policies once you leave ours.

7. Children

This site is written for a family audience and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, children included.

8. Your rights

Since we hold no personal data about site visitors, there is usually nothing to access or delete. If analytics were enabled and you have questions about data held by Google or Meta, their privacy tools govern that data; we can point you in the right direction if you call.

9. Changes

If our practices change — for example, if analytics are switched on — this page will be updated first, and the consent banner will always ask before anything new runs.

Effective date: to be set at launch.
